About us

'Astral International SAL' provides horticultural investors a complete package for setting a hi-tech floriculture project.

Our services are backed by wealth of knowledge and practical experience. We pride ourselves in offering low cost solutions and indigenous technology for setting up the project.

Our team of experts looks into all key aspects; The services would include 'conceptualization' of project, feasibility study, identification and sourcing of material and setting up suitably designed greenhouses and infrastructure.

The technical support includes Crop management and cultivation techniques, Production and post harvest management, market information on trends and new varieties will be always provided together with on job training.
